At just 19 years old, Alex Chen has already made significant waves in the indie game development scene. Starting with simple Unity projects at age 14, Alex quickly developed a passion for creating immersive worlds that blend nostalgic pixel art with modern gameplay mechanics. After dropping out of computer science studies to pursue game development full-time, Alex founded Neon Pixel Studios from their bedroom in Portland, Oregon.

Alex's breakout title "Chrono Glitch" - a time-manipulation platformer with a synthwave aesthetic - garnered over 500,000 downloads and was featured in multiple indie game festivals. Known for meticulous attention to detail and innovative mechanics, Alex continues to push boundaries while maintaining a distinctly personal artistic vision. When not coding, Alex can be found composing chiptune music or mentoring young developers through online workshops.
